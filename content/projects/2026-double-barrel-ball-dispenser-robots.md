## Overview

I designed this robot around two primary requirements: the ability to hold 15 balls and the ability to collapse to under a 12" total height to go under certain field elements. In combination, those requirements eliminated the competition-standard hopper style robot due to its height. As a result, I invented a unique power sharing mechanism to power four roller sections with only two motors involving four pneumatically driven clutches and two ratchets.
